About

Tawsila is a Carpooling application (also known as ride sharing or car sharing) is a straightforward and practical concept: some people share a ride so that more than one person travels in a car. The advantages are fantastic, and if you’re already a user, you know everything about them. If that’s not the case, you should be aware of what you’re missing. After finding out, you might join the network.

Our vision is to become the world’s most used and most trustable carpooling application.

Our mission is that the application’s clever use helps to exceed users’ expectations, this will improve people’s lives.

How It works

1. Send Rides

When the passengers need a ride they send a request to the available drivers.

2. Recive Requests

Drivers receive requests and accept the suitable requests.

3. Trip Ended

Both driver and passenger must press ending trip. The passenger gets a receipt for the cost on his application, after accepting the cash will transfer from his wallet to driver’s wallet.

4. Passenger review

After the trip ended your review can help us and other passengers.

Safety Insurance

Share Location

you must feel comfortable riding or driving at day or night. So you can share loved ones where you’re going, someone to turn to if anything happens.

Trip Recording

All Tawsila rides are tracked from start to finish, so there’s a record of your trip if something happens.

Two Way Rating

Your feedback matters. Low-rated trips are logged, and users may be removed to protect Tawsila community.

Ride Check

Using sensors and GPS data, RideCheck can help detect if a trip has an unexpected long stop. If so, we'll check on you and offer tools to get help.
